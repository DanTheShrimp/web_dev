One Example: Legos

One great way for me to improve things is to make things out of legos and make them better as a I get new ideas. I have made a laptop stand currently, and as I need new things for it I can add them, making a better laptop stand. Some of the current features that I have added are: a holder for a powered hub, a little container for adapters and my 64 GB thumb drive, and some pillars and sections underneath to route cords for better cable management.
One of the main things limiting me right now is the amount of legos I have. If I had more then I could keep going, adding more and more things to my laptop stand making it better and better. So far the best course of action is to keep adding more
