I like Taynix

Taynix are from the Skyward series by Brandon Sanderson, and are the custest things to ever leave factories. Taynix are (not so, they are the size of a loaf of bread) little slugs who love to eat mushrooms and caviar, hang on your shoulder, and are just cute in general. There are 8 known different types of Taynix, and each one has a special power.

Known powers
The yellow and blue ones are hyperslugs and can teleport
The red and black ones are bladeslugs and can shoot mindblades that can cut anything
The orange and purple ones are commslugs and can mantain long distance communication
The light blue and green slugs are inhibitor slugs and can put up a shield that can inhibit other cytonic powers
The rainbow slugs are illusion slugs and can create illusions and alter their appearance
Unkown powers

Blue-glowing blackish slugs
